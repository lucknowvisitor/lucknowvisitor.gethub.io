SINGAPORE MALL

HISTORY

Singapore Mall, an extensive themed mall, the first of its kind in Lucknow, offers you close to 1.5 lakh sq.ft. of total extravaganza and fun. As the name signifies, Singapore is the theme of the space with beautifully set up shops and sidewalk cafes, also showcasing high end electronic equipments, Italian flooring, automated washrooms, modern toilets, valet parking, free Wi-Fi, 24 hrs security and the finest in safety norms. You will find at Singapore Mall, a varied concoction of shopping options that include the most popular in national and international brands of clothing, home products, personal care merchandise, hypermarket, ethnic & regional showcases, electronics and much more. Also check out Singapore Mall range of restaurants, cafes and the food court. Most importantly, Singapore Mall brings the finest in entertainment to you with their first ever 4K HD theatre in the city with 3 theatres & 687 seats. Kids have their own entertainment zone and you could also relax in their interesting multipurpose performance area or the premiere night club rocking the city.

Singapore mall is situated in the city of Lucknow, India. Spanning 1 acre and with a total leasable area of 1.5 Lac square feet, it is the first theme based mall in Lucknow with a concept of high end shopping. Deriving a theme of Singapore, Italian flooring, Electronic equipments, Automatic washrooms, High end toilets and Fire like safety norms makes Singapore mall one of the best theme based malls in India. You can Shop, dine and watch a movie all under one roof. And that's not all you can do here, there are also eateries for the foodies, cinemas for the movie buffs, and lots of shopping bargains for the shopaholics. The Singapore mall has come up with the first 4K HD theatre in Lucknow with 3 theatres comprising 687 seats. It has 66 Gold lounge seats (Maximum in Lucknow), best in class Business seats and Economy seats.
