BASKET CHAT

We all love chats, be it pani ke batashe (pani puri), aloo tikki, dahi bhalla or papri chat! The people of Lucknow are no exception. They enjoy their fair share of chats on a regular basis. But if we have to name one special food in Lucknow in the chat category, it has to be basket chat. The dish goes as it sounds, chat served in a basket. Here chat means a mix of mashed aloo tikki, peas and different chutneys and basket means a potato shelling fried in the shape of a basket. Yes, it is unique and it tastes pretty amazing! Where to taste it? At Gomti Nagar.

A jovial person in his characteristic Gandhi topi, apron and a neat french cut, Mauryaji is often surrounded by people requesting selfies. He is a wonderful person to talk to, so whenever you are at Royal Cafe, meet him and give my reference I’m sure he’d love to dish out some Basket Chaat for you too
