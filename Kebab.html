KEBAB ROLL

Ruling the streets of Lucknow for ages, kebabs are something, that is very sacred to the city and definitely the best street food in Lucknow. Your trip to this city won’t be complete without trying these lip-smacking kebabs. A crispy outer layer with a soft filling inside, these kebabs are cooked until they are tender enough to dissolve in your mouth. Best complemented with rumaali roti, one can also find veg options for this delicacy. Galouti Kebabs, Tundey Kebabs, and Veg Shami kebab are some of the must-try. Best Place to try: Tunday Kababi

Cost ₹50 per plate
